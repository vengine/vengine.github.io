Welcome to Block Puzzle in Space!

In 20xx, a huge array of blocks appears in outer space close to Earth. Is this the Apocalypse or the Revelation? The secret will be revealed by you.
"Block Puzzle in Space" is a puzzle game. players can place blocks in the scene. if the blocks fill a row or column, the row or column will be eliminated.
Players earn scores by eliminating blocks. when the scores accumulate to a certain amount, the level will be cleared.
However, every time the blocks are eliminated, UFO blocks will be randomly dropped from space to hinder the player.
The higher the level, the more UFO blocks will be dropped, and accordingly the challenge will become higher and higher.
"Block Puzzle in Space" has a total of 24 levels. no one knows what will happen after all the levels are cleared. the challenge is waiting for you!
